BiteNoted Features

Your memories are more useful when they’re tied to places.

Fast Journaling, Built For Repeats

Rich Entries

Capture a meal (or any item type) with title, visit date, rating, notes, tags, favorites, and place details.

Photos

Attach one or many photos to an entry, then browse them later in a dedicated “Your Photos” gallery with filters.

Places

A dedicated Places view groups entries by venue automatically. Favorite places bubble to the top and are easy to filter, and each place has its own timeline + map preview with quick “Open In Maps”.

Map-First By Default

See your history as pins and quickly jump back to “that spot”. Drop a pin anywhere, or search nearby places and attach venue details.

At Home Shortcut

Optional “At Home” shortcut to start entries without searching for a venue.

Backups You Can Trust

Export entries as JSON, plus full backup/restore as a ZIP (including photos when available).

Find Anything Later

Search

Basic search across your entry content and place info.

Filters

Filter down what you’re looking at (type, tags, rating, location, date range, favorites). Advanced search/filters are a Pro feature.

Photos With Context

Photo detail keeps the “where” alongside the image (place/address + map).

Saved Searches (Pro)

Save searches so your favorite views are one tap away.

Private By Design

Your journal lives on-device, with optional iCloud sync to your private database when signed in. Tracking is optional (only for personalized ads on the Free plan). No third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs.

iCloud Sync

Optional iCloud sync to your private database when you’re signed in to iCloud.

Pro Power-Ups

Templates

Reusable “starter entries” you can quick-add when you’re in a hurry. Create templates from an entry or directly from a place.

Insights

Monthly recaps, trends, top tags/places, favorites counts, and map-based views.

Widgets

Widgets for recent entries, “on this day”, stats summaries, and quick-add templates.

Quick Capture

Quick Add from Lock Screen / Action Button to capture in the moment.

Privacy Lock

Optional privacy lock (“Lock On Return”) using Face ID / Touch ID.

All plans include core journaling, exports/backups, and optional iCloud sync. Pricing is a one-time in-app purchase, not a subscription. Free includes ads, No Ads removes them, and Pro unlocks advanced features like saved searches, templates, widgets, insights, quick capture, and privacy lock.
