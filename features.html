BiteNoted Features

A map-first food journal for logging meals, photos, and places.

Everyday journaling

Rich entries

Unlimited entries for every plan, with title, item type, visit date, ratings, notes, tags, favorites, and place details.

Photos

Add photos to entries, then browse them later in a dedicated photos grid with filters.

Places

Your entries automatically group into a places list, with each place showing its own map preview and entry history.

Maps first

Browse entries as pins, long-press to drop a new pin, and attach venue details from map search.

Quick actions

From an entry you can open a place in Apple Maps, visit a venue website, or call the location when available.

Import and export

Export entries or full backups, and import them later if you ever want to restore your journal.

Search and organization

Search

Find entries fast with search across titles, notes, tags, places, and locations.

Filters

Sort by date or rating and filter favorites to quickly narrow what you want to revisit.

Photo context

Photo detail view shows where you were and ties images back to the entry and place.

iCloud sync

Sync is included with every plan when you are signed in to iCloud.

All plans include unlimited entries and iCloud sync. Pricing is a one-time purchase, not a subscription.
