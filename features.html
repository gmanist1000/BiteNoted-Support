BiteNoted Features

A map-first food journal for logging meals, photos, and places.

Everyday Journaling

Rich Entries

Unlimited entries, with title, item type, visit date, ratings, notes, tags, favorites, place details, and location.

Photos

Add multiple photos to entries, then browse them later in a dedicated photos grid with filters and count badges.

Places

Your entries automatically group into a places list, with each place showing its own map preview, entry history with filters, Open in Maps, and favorite place pins.

Maps First

Browse entries as pins, long-press to drop a new pin, and attach venue details from map search.

Quick Actions

Entry detail quick actions and deep links to open a place in Apple Maps, visit a venue website, or call the location when available.

Import and Export

Export JSON entries or full ZIP backups with photos, then import later with optional dedupe.

Search and Organization

Search

Find entries fast with basic search across titles, notes, tags, places, and locations.

Filters

Sort by date or rating, filter favorite entries or places, and unlock advanced filters with Pro.

Photo Context

Photo detail view shows the place, address, and map and ties images back to the entry.

iCloud Sync

Sync is included with every plan when you are signed in to iCloud.

All plans include unlimited entries and iCloud sync. Pricing is a one-time in-app purchase, not a subscription. Free includes ads, No Ads removes them, and Pro unlocks all gated features. Pro includes future Pro features and app updates at no extra cost.
