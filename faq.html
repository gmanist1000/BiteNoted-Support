FAQ / Help

Quick answers for the most common BiteNoted questions.

iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is available on every plan. You can toggle it in the BiteNoted Settings screen.

Common troubleshooting steps

  • Confirm you are signed in to iCloud in iOS Settings and iCloud Drive is enabled.
  • Check that BiteNoted has iCloud enabled in iOS Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.
  • Open the app on both devices while on Wi-Fi and wait a few minutes for sync to catch up.
  • Make sure Low Power Mode is off and Background App Refresh is enabled.
  • If sync still looks stuck, toggle iCloud Sync off and on in BiteNoted Settings.

Map and Place Search Quirks

BiteNoted uses Apple MapKit for maps and place search. Results can vary by region and may take time to update after a venue changes name or address.

  • If a place is missing, try searching by street address or a nearby landmark.
  • Short names can be ambiguous; try adding the city or neighborhood.
  • Map results are cached by iOS, so reopening the app can refresh results.

Import, Export, and Backups

You can export entries or create a full backup (zip) from BiteNoted Settings. Exports can be saved to Files, AirDropped, or stored in a cloud drive.

  • Exports are best for sharing or working with your data outside the app.
  • Full backups are best for restoring your entire journal later.
  • Imports are available in Settings and can restore a prior backup.

Widgets

Widgets are part of the Pro plan. Add them from the iOS widget gallery, then open BiteNoted once so the widget can load data.

  • If a widget looks blank, open BiteNoted and wait a few seconds.
  • Privacy Lock can hide widget content when enabled.

Privacy Lock

Privacy Lock uses Face ID or Touch ID to lock the app. It also hides widget content when enabled.

  • If biometrics are disabled on the device, Privacy Lock will fall back to the device passcode.
  • After restarting your device, iOS may require a passcode before Face ID or Touch ID is available.

Ads and Tracking (ATT)

The free plan shows ads. The tracking prompt appears only on the free plan and only if ads are enabled. You can allow or deny tracking in the iOS prompt.

  • Allowing tracking enables personalized ads.
  • Denying tracking shows non-personalized ads.
  • Purchasing No Ads or Pro removes ads and the tracking prompt.

Restore Purchases

To restore purchases, open BiteNoted Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

If restore does not complete, wait a moment and try again. Apple purchases can occasionally take a minute to sync.

No Ads vs Pro

No Ads removes ads from the free plan. Pro unlocks advanced features like full-text search, widgets, Privacy Lock, templates, and future Pro updates.
